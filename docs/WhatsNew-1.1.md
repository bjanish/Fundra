# What's New — Version 1.1

## App Store "What's New" Text

- Deleting your last recorded month now gracefully resets the app, so you can start fresh anytime — with a clear warning before it happens.
- Rename accounts directly from the Edit Balance screen — just tap the pencil icon.
- Monthly reminder notification on the 1st of each month to keep you on track.
- Up to 6 accounts supported, with smarter chart labels that adapt to your account count.
- UI enhancements to the charts and buttons for a cleaner, more polished look.
