# Privacy Policy

**Fundra** — developed by Janish Labs

**Last updated:** June 13, 2026

## Summary

Fundra does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information you enter stays on your device.

## Data Collection

Fundra collects **no data**. Specifically:

- No personal information
- No usage analytics
- No crash reports
- No cookies or tracking
- No account creation required
- No internet connection required

## Data Storage

All data you enter (account names, balances, dates) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is never transmitted to any server, third party, or external service.

## Third-Party Services

Fundra uses **no third-party services**, SDKs, or analytics tools.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data with anyone because we do not have access to any data.

## Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security (Face ID, Touch ID, or passcode). Fundra uses Apple's LocalAuthentication framework to verify your identity before granting access to the app.

## Children's Privacy

Fundra does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
