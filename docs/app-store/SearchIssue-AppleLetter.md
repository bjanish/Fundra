# Apple Developer Support — Search Issue

## When to Send
If "Fundra" still autocorrects to "Tundra" in App Store search 3+ days after a version goes live.

## How to File
Apple Developer → Account → Contact Us → App Store → App Discovery/Search

## Sample Message

---

Subject: App Store search autocorrects my app name "Fundra" to "Tundra"

Hi,

My app "Fundra - Savings Tracker" (Apple ID: [your app ID]) is not appearing in App Store search results when users type "Fundra". Instead, the search autocorrects to "Tundra" and shows unrelated results.

Details:
- App name: Fundra - Savings Tracker
- The app was previously searchable by typing "Fundra" directly
- After submitting version 1.2 on June 27, 2026, the search stopped working
- Users must type "Fundra " (with a trailing space) or tap "Search for 'Fundra' instead" to find the app
- The app does appear if you add a space or apostrophe after the word

This appears to be the search autocorrect treating "Fundra" as a misspelling of "Tundra" rather than recognizing it as a valid app name.

Could you please investigate and correct the search indexing for my app? I'd appreciate any guidance on resolving this.

Thank you,
Brian Janish

---

## Notes
- Include your Apple ID / app ID from App Store Connect
- Keep it factual and concise — Apple support responds better to specific, reproducible issues
- If they ask for screenshots, show the autocorrect behavior vs. the space workaround
