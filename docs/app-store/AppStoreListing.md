## Subtitle (30 chars max)

## Description

Fundra is a simple, private savings tracker. Record your account balances, visualize your progress, and watch your savings grow — all without ever leaving your device.

No accounts to create. No bank connections. No data sent anywhere. Just you and your numbers.

FEATURES

• Track multiple savings accounts in one place
• Beautiful bar chart shows your balances at a glance
• Record monthly snapshots and see growth over time
• Growth Summary with line chart and percentage tracking
• Export your data as CSV or save your chart as an image
• Face ID / Touch ID protection
• Dark mode support
• Motivational quotes to keep you going

PRIVACY FIRST

Your data stays on your device. Fundra never connects to the internet, never collects analytics, and never sees your financial information. What you save is yours alone.
